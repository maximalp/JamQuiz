JamQuiz

JamQuiz is simple. Click on a quiz below and the challenge will appear.

Press the play button. You have 30 seconds to guess the song playing by typing the name of the song in the guess box. You'll see your guesses along the way. You can earn a maximum of 300 points for each song, but you lose ten points for every second you fail to guess correctly. Skip forward if anxious.

Good luck!
