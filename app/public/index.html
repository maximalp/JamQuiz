JamQuiz

Jam Quiz is simple. Choose between two quizzes below based on the music style you want to play with. After choosing the quiz, you'll be sent to the game!

To really start playing JamQuiz, click the play button. You'll have 30 seconds to guess the song playing by typing the name of the song in the guess box. You'll see your guesses along the way. You can get a maximum of 300 points for one song, but you lose ten points every second you fail to guess correclty.

Good luck!
